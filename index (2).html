"I Am Watching You" by Teresa Driscoll is a psychological thriller about guilt, secrets, and consequences. The story centers on a woman, Ella, who notices two young men flirting with teenage girls on a train and decides not to intervene. The next day, one of the girls goes missing. Wracked with guilt, Ella becomes entangled in a web of lies, as the novel explores the perspectives of various characters linked to the disappearance. The narrative keeps readers guessing with twists and turns, questioning who can be trusted.
